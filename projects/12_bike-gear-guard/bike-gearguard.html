Bike Gear Guard Upgrade

Personal Project — December 2020

×

The gear guard on my bike broke off one day, so I decided I would design a new one for it. I modelled the renovated gear guard in SolidWorks and printed it on my Ender 3 Pro in PLA.

The eight designs I presented in the survey

I drew a couple of different designs and surveyed everyone in my social circles, asking "which one would look coolest on my bike?" After receiving the results, I took some measurements, modelled the preferred one, printed it out, and attached it to my bike with some screws.
